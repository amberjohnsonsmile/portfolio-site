About

With a background in art and music, I bring a creative edge to full stack web development. I specialize in making websites with Ember and React, as well as native apps with React Native. I love backend work too, especially integrating servers, databases, and APIs.

My experience covers techniques such as pair programming, test driven development, and Agile workflow. In my free time, I enjoy playing the cello, hanging out with my cat, and thru-hiking long-distance trails, including the Pacific Crest Trail and the Colorado Trail.
